About

Here is a timeline of our most significant milestones!

  • July 2020

    Beginnings

    Conservation of Wildlife is important to protect the endangered species along with their natural habitat. The main concern was to preserve the habitats for the future generations of wildlife.

  • August 2020

    Project Rhino

    Our first campaign was based at Kaziranga! A large population of greater one-horned rhinos is found in Assam, India, which is a result of years of impactful conservation in the state. 

  • September 2020

    Global Expansion

    With immense effort and support we are now a globally credible NGO working on the ground in more than 100 cities across the globe.

  • October 2020

    1000+ Members

    With our mission is to conserve nature and reduce the most pressing threats to the diversity of life on Earth, we are receiving support from over thousand members and counting!
